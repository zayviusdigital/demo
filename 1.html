WebView can also assist with common browsing metaphors, such as history list of visited URLs to support backwards and forwards navigation. Still WebView comes with its own set of cons such as it’s a much more expensive widget to use, in terms of memory consumption than a TextView. The reason for this increased memory is because WebView is powered by WebKit/Blink that are open source Web rendering engine to power content in browsers like Chrome.
